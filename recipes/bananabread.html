Chocolate Chip Banana Bread

This dense, delicious banana bread will have your friends and family begging for more!

Description

Banana bread is a simple baked good, but often it can be dry and lack flavour. Our recipe changes that up with some simple tweaks that will take your banana bread to a whole new level! The key differences here are using more bananas, and using overripe bananas. You'll want 5 to 6 bananas that are turning brown, then store them in the freezer until you're ready to bake. When the peel is dark brown all over, they are ready for baking! This also means the flesh inside will be gooey and easier work with.

Indredients

  • 5-6 overripe bananas
  • 1/3 cup (75g) butter, melted
  • ½ cup sugar(100 g)
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • salt, to taste
  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour(185 g)
  • ½ cup mini chocolate chips(85 g)

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350˚F (180˚C).
  2. In a bowl, add the bananas and whisk until smooth. Add in the melted butter and stir until well combined.
  3. Add the sugar, egg, vanilla, baking soda, salt, and flour, and stir until the batter is smooth.
  4. Add in the chocolate chips and pour the batter into a greased loaf pan. Top with additional chocolate chips.
  5. Bake for 50 minutes to an hour, or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  6. Cool completely before serving.
